Features

Below are some common features in the management system that we provide for any small and medium scale businesses, who maynot yet know that they need a system in place to grow further.

Order Management

This feature supports managing orders, creating or booking new orders, calender view to see how many orders in a particular day of month etc.

  • Manage orders, create or edit orders, calender view to see overall orders in a month, detail view of orders in a day etc
  • Manage order status, notifications
  • Manage orderable items, item rate, item availability, overall items, add/ edit items etc

Business Insights

This feature provides multiple charts and analytical data and statistics data to see business trend and helps to take business decisions. Below are some basic questions that can be answered with this data.

  • What is the yearly turn over, profit, ecpenditure etc ?
  • How much are you spending in say transport/ delivery or for salary of employees?
  • Which item is getting sold the most?
  • How many orders do you expect in the coming month?

Payment Management

This feature provides a way to deal with payment of any order in a simple way.

  • How much should he pay after he added the additional item at last minute ?
  • How much discount are you giving him in this order ?
  • What are the pending payment orders which are already delivered ?

Expenditure Management

Expenditure is one of the crutial aspect that a business man should be aware of, how much are you spending in what category is important.

This gives clear picture of how much exactly is being spent on actual raw material/ groceries, equipments, transport, additional supplies, salary of employees etc.
